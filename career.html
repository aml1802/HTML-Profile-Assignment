The Rock – Career

Home Career Legacy

From the University of Miami and the CFL to WWE superstardom and a dominant film career.

Football Years

Johnson played defensive tackle for the Miami Hurricanes, winning a national championship in 1991 and learning resilience after losing his starting spot.

WWE Era

Debuting in 1996, he developed the “The Rock” persona, winning multiple world titles and becoming one of the most recognizable performers in sports entertainment.

Hollywood Transition

Beginning with The Mummy Returns and The Scorpion King, Johnson transitioned into acting and built a career across franchises like Fast & Furious and Jumanji.
