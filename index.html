Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson

Home Career Legacy

Dwayne Douglas Johnson, known globally as “The Rock,” is an actor, producer, and former professional wrestler. A proud University of Miami alumnus, he combines discipline and charisma to become one of Hollywood’s most successful figures.

Early Life and the U

Born in Hayward, California, Johnson attended the University of Miami on a full football scholarship. He played defensive tackle and was part of the 1991 National Championship team.

From Wrestling Rings to Hollywood Screens

Following a brief stint in the CFL, Johnson entered professional wrestling in 1996. Under the WWE banner, he became “The Rock,” known for electrifying charisma and unmatched presence.

Key Achievements

  1. 8× WWE World Champion
  2. Star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame (2017)
  3. Time 100 Most Influential People (2016 & 2019)
  4. Highest-paid actor worldwide (multiple years)
  5. University of Miami Sports Hall of Fame inductee
